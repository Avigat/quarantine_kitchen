CHEESY VEGETABLE PIZZA

DETAILS:

  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes.
  • Cooking Time: 6 minutes.
  • Makes 2 pizzas.
  • Baking Temperature: 200°C (400°F).
  • Baking Time: 15 to 17 minutes.

INGREDIENTS:

For The Cheese Sauce:

  • ½ cup grated processed cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp plain flour (maida)
  • ¼ cup milk
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper (kalimirch) to taste

For The Vegetable Topping:

  • ½ cup sliced and blanched baby corn
  • ½ cup blanched carrot cubes
  • ½ cup blanched zucchini cubes
  • ½ cup sliced red capsicum
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper (kalimirch) to taste

Other Ingredients:

  • 2 thin crust pizza base (7")
  • ½ cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup pizza sauce

RECIPE:

  1. Place a pizza base on a clean, dry surface and spread a portion of the cheese sauce and arrange 1 portion of the vegetable topping evenly over it.
  2. Spread ¼ cup of pizza sauce evenly over it and finally sprinkle ¼ cup of cheese over it.
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 to make 1 more pizza.
  4. Place both the pizzas on a greased baking tray and bake in a pre-heated oven at 200°C (400°F) for 10 to 12 minutes or till the base is evenly browned and the cheese melts.
  5. Cut into equal wedges and serve immediately.

GARLIC NOODLES

DETAILS:

  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 3 minutes
  • Serves 2

INGREDIENTS:

  • 2 tbsp chopped garlic (lehsun)
  • 2 cups boiled hakka noodles
  • ½ tsp dry red chilli flakes (paprika)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped coriander (dhania)
  • Salt to taste

RECIPE:

  1. Heat the oil in a broad non-stick pan, add the garlic and sauté on a medium flame for 30 seconds.
  2. Add all the remaining ingredients, toss gently and cook on a medium flame for 2 minutes, while stirring occasionally.
  3. Serve immediately.

ALOO TIKKI

DETAILS:

  • Preparation Time: 5 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 10 minutes
  • Makes 10 Tikkis

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup boiled, peeled and mashed potatoes
  • ½ cup boiled and coarsely crushed green peas
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped coriander (dhania)
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 1½ tsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for greasing and cooking

RECIPE:

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Divide the mixture into 6 equal portions and shape each portion into a round, flat 50 mm tikki.
  3. Heat a non-stick tava (griddle) on a medium flame and grease it with oil and cook the tikkis, using little oil, till both sides are golden brown in colour.
  4. Serve hot with tomato ketchup or green chutney.
