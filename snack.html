FRENCH FRIES

DETAILS:

  • Preparation Time: 5 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 20 minutes
  • Makes 1¼ cups

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1¼ cups potato fingers
  • 2 tsp oil
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp roasted cumin seeds (jeera) powder
  • Salt to taste

RECIPE:

  1. Combine the potatoes and enough water in a deep non-stick pan and cook on a medium flame for 5 to 7 minutes. Drain well and keep aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a broad non-stick pan, add the potato fingers and cook on a medium flame for 8 to 10 minutes, while tossing occasionally.
  3. Add the chilli powder, cumin seeds powder and salt, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 1 to 2 minutes, while stirring occasionally.
  4. Serve with tomato ketchap

VEGETABLE SANDWICH

DETAILS:

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Makes 4 Sandwiches

INGREDIENTS:

  • 8 bread slices
  • 4 tsp butter for spreading
  • 2 tsp butter for brushing
  • 2 tsp butter for cooking
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 1½ tsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for greasing and cooking

For The Stuffing

  • ½ cup finely chopped onions
  • ½ cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped coriander (dhania)
  • ¼ cup grated processed cheese
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp coriander (dhania) powder
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds (jeera) powder
  • Salt to taste

RECIPE:

  1. Divide the stuffing into 4 equal portions.
  2. Place 2 bread slices on a clean, dry surface and spread ½ tsp of butter evenly over each slice.
  3. Place a portion of the stuffing on one buttered bread slice and spread it evenly.
  4. Sandwich it using another bread slice with the buttered side facing downwards and press it lightly.
  5. Brush ½ tsp of butter evenly over it.
  6. Heat a non-stick a tava (griddle), put ½ tsp of butter for cooking and place the sandwich on it with the buttered side facing upwards and cook on a medium flame till it turns golden brown in color from both the sides.
  7. Cut the sandwich and serve with tomato ketchap.
  8. Repeat steps 2 to 7 to make 3 more sandwiches

MANGO SEV PURI

DETAILS:

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking Time: Nil
  • Makes 18 mango sev puris

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 cup finely chopped mangoes (Totapuri)
  • 18 papdi
  • ½ cup finely chopped capsicum
  • ½ cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander (dhania)
  • ½ tsp finely chopped green chillies
  • 2 tsp chaat masala
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 1½ tsp dried mango powder (amchur)
  • Sev for topping

RECIPE:

  1. Combine the mangoes, capsicum, tomatoes, coriander, green chillies. chaat masala, chilli powder and dried mango powder in a deep bowl and mix well.
  2. Place 9 papdis on a serving plate and top each papdi with 1 tsp of the prepared mango mixture.
  3. Repeat steps 2 and 3 to make 9 more mango sev puris.
  4. Serve immediately
